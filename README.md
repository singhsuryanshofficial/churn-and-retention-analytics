## ğŸ“Œ Key Insights

- The bank exhibits a **high churn rate**, indicating significant customer attrition.
- Churn is concentrated among **inactive customers**, **low-balance segments**, **low credit score groups**, and specific products and regions.
- Retention is strongest among **active customers with mid-level tenure and higher balances**, particularly for select products and geographies.
- Customer activity and account value are consistently associated with higher retention stability.

---

## ğŸ¯ Recommendations

- Implement **early-stage engagement initiatives** during critical tenure periods.
- Introduce **activity-based alerts and re-engagement campaigns** for inactive customers.
- Improve underperforming products by adopting best practices from high-retention offerings.
- Apply **region-specific retention strategies** in high-churn markets.
- Design **value-based loyalty programs** for high-balance customers.
